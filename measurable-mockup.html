📈 Mind OS — Measurable Goals + first-run onboarding (concept mockup)

Borrowing the OKR/KPI "measurable" pattern (typed target, start to target, cadence) and pairing it with timeline-aware progress: the percentage is computed from how far you have moved toward the target, then checked against how much time has passed. No more guessing on a slider, and a brand-new member always knows the next thing to do.

🟢 On track / ahead: progress ≥ time elapsed 🟠 Behind: progress lags the clock Formula: progress% = (start − current) ÷ (start − target) · pace = progress% vs time% elapsed
