How resilience works (BIT-N)

Resilience is your ability to bounce back and keep going when life pushes on you. Like a muscle, you build it by working it. BIT-N helps you spot the four things that hold you back, so you can train the muscle that beats each one.
🧱 Block — a belief or fear that stops you before you start ("I'm not good enough"). The muscle: honest self-talk, seeing the thought clearly and choosing a truer one.
⚡ Impulse — an urge to do the easy thing now that hurts you later (scrolling, snapping, snacking). The muscle: the pause, noticing the urge and riding it out instead of obeying it.
🎯 Trigger — a person, place, or feeling that sets you off (stress, a notification, 9pm). The muscle: awareness, knowing your triggers so you can plan around them.
💧 Need — something missing you keep filling in unhealthy ways (rest, connection, purpose). The muscle: naming the real need and meeting it in a good way.
Name them and you can train them. Every skill in Mind OS is a rep for one of these muscles, and the more honestly you name what holds you back, the more precisely we can help.
