The framework

What is the Q4Score?

Q4 = Quality of life, across 4 pillars. It's our simple way to make something as big as your life small enough to see and move. Rate each pillar out of 100. The journey is to keep striving toward 100, with kindness for where you are today.
PILLAR 01

Purpose & Meaning

Living with intention, meaning, and real impact.

PILLAR 02

Relationships & Belonging

Connection with self and others, with honesty and presence.

PILLAR 03

Mind & Body

Physical and emotional wellbeing, resilience, strength.

PILLAR 04

Money & Mastery

Career on track, skills growing, wealth goals in sight.
